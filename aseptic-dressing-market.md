<p><p>The aseptic dressing market is categorized primarily into two types: transparent and non-woven. Transparent dressings are made of a clear, adhesive material that allows for easy monitoring of the wound without removing the dressing, providing both visibility and protection. Non-woven dressings, on the other hand, are crafted from synthetic fibers and are designed to absorb exudate effectively while promoting a moist healing environment. Both types are essential for preventing infection and facilitating faster healing in surgical and wound care applications.</p></p>
