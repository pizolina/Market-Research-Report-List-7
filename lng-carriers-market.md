<p><p>LNG carriers are specialized ships designed to transport liquefied natural gas, and they primarily come in two market types: Moss Type and Membrane Type. Moss Type carriers feature independent spherical tanks, allowing for greater storage flexibility and stability but are generally bulkier. In contrast, Membrane Type carriers utilize thin membranes bonded to the hull, optimizing cargo space and enhancing fuel efficiency. Each design offers distinct advantages, catering to varying market demands and operational requirements in the LNG shipping industry.</p></p>
