<p><p>Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) involves various techniques to extract additional oil from reservoirs. CO2 Enhanced Oil Recovery uses carbon dioxide to increase pressure and reduce oil viscosity. Nitrogen Enhanced Oil Recovery employs nitrogen to improve oil mobility. Polymer Flooding introduces polymers to increase water viscosity and sweep efficiency. Surfactant Flooding reduces interfacial tension, enhancing oil displacement. Alkaline Flooding alters rock wettability, optimizing recovery. Micellar Flooding utilizes surfactants to form micelles, capturing more oil. Other methods include thermal and microbial enhanced recovery techniques.</p></p>
