<p><p>The cooling coils market is categorized into three primary types: water cooling coils, steam cooling coils, and refrigerant cooling coils. Water cooling coils use water as a heat exchange medium, effective in HVAC systems for temperature control. Steam cooling coils utilize steam to cool air by condensing the steam, offering efficient cooling in industrial applications. Refrigerant cooling coils operate using refrigerants, providing precise temperature regulation in refrigeration and air conditioning systems, catering to diverse commercial and residential needs.</p></p>
