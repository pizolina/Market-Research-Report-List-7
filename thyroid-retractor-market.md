<p><ul><li>Hand-held Retractor</li><li>Automatic Retractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thyroid retractor market consists of two primary types: hand-held retractors and automatic retractors. Hand-held retractors are manually operated instruments used by surgeons to maintain exposure during thyroid surgeries, offering significant control. In contrast, automatic retractors use a mechanical or electronic system to provide consistent tension and positioning, reducing the surgeon's workload and enhancing precision. Both types are essential in thyroid procedures, contributing to patient safety and optimal surgical outcomes through improved visibility and access to the surgical site.</p></p>
