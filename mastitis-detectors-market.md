<p><p>The mastitis detectors market can be categorized into two main types: mounted detectors and portable detectors. Mounted detectors are typically installed on milking equipment, providing continuous monitoring of udder health during the milking process. They offer real-time data and are designed for efficient integration into existing systems. Portable detectors, on the other hand, are handheld devices that allow for on-the-spot testing and evaluation of individual animals, offering flexibility and convenience for farmers to assess udder health outside of milking operations.</p></p>
