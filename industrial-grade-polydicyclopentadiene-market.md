<p><p>The industrial-grade polydicyclopentadiene market is characterized by several key processing types. Resin Transfer Molding (RTM) entails injecting resin into a mold containing dry fibers, allowing for high-strength parts. Vacuum Assisted Resin Infusion (VARI) utilizes vacuum pressure to draw resin into a fiber preform, optimizing resin distribution. Other methods may include compression molding and extrusion, each catering to specific applications and material properties.</p></p>
