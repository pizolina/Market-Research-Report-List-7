<p><p>The cannabis extract market consists of various extraction methods, each with unique advantages. Sub/Supercritical CO2 Extraction efficiently preserves purity and potency using pressurized carbon dioxide, while Ethanol Extraction is effective for large-scale production but may compromise flavor. Hydrocarbon Extraction utilizes solvents like butane, known for producing potent concentrates but requiring safety precautions. Solvent-less Extraction involves techniques like rosin pressing, avoiding chemicals altogether. Other methods include cold-water extraction and various hybrid techniques, catering to diverse consumer preferences and product types.</p></p>
