<p><p>Roller seam welding machines are categorized based on their seam types. Circumferential seam welding machines are designed for welding around cylindrical parts, ideal for tanks and pipes. Longitudinal seam welding machines are used for welding along the length of components, suitable for tubes and ducts. Corner seam welding machines effectively join components at right angles, essential in manufacturing enclosures and frames. Other types may include specialized or multifunctional machines that cater to various welding applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
