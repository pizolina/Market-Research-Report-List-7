<p><p>The contraceptive market can be categorized into three main types: prolonged contraception, short-term contraception, and emergency contraception. Prolonged contraception includes methods like implants and IUDs that offer long-lasting protection, typically for several years. Short-term contraception features options like pills, patches, and injections, which provide coverage for shorter periods, usually monthly or weekly. Emergency contraception is designed for use after unprotected intercourse to prevent pregnancy, with options like pill formulations that are effective within a limited timeframe.</p></p>
