<p><p>The bulk cocoa market encompasses three main products: cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. Cocoa liquor, produced from ground cocoa beans, serves as a base for chocolate products. Cocoa butter, extracted fat from the beans, is crucial for confectionery, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, offering texture and creaminess. Cocoa powder, obtained after fat removal, is a key ingredient in baking and beverages, providing rich flavor and color. Together, these products are essential in various industries, primarily food and cosmetics.</p></p>
