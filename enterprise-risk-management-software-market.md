<p><p>Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ERM software is hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, easier updates, and accessibility from any location with internet access. This model minimizes IT overhead and supports collaborative risk management. In contrast, on-premises ERM software is installed locally on the organization's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, but often requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type suits different organizational needs and resources.</p></p>
