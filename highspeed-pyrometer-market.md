<p><p>The high-speed pyrometer market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact pyrometers. Contact pyrometers measure temperature by directly touching the object, providing accurate readings for specific applications. In contrast, non-contact pyrometers use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, ideal for high-speed or hazardous environments where direct contact is impractical. Both types cater to various industries, including manufacturing and metallurgy, ensuring precise temperature monitoring for quality control and process optimization.</p></p>
