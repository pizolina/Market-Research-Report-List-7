<p><p>In the electronic devices market, wireless antennas are crucial for communication. UHF (Ultra High Frequency) antennas operate between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, making them ideal for television broadcasts, mobile phones, and two-way radios due to their ability to transmit over longer distances with better penetration. VHF (Very High Frequency) antennas, operating between 30 MHz and 300 MHz, are commonly used for FM radio, TV, and public service communications. Both types are essential for efficient wireless connectivity in various applications.</p></p>
