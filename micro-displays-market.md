<p><ul><li>LCD</li><li>LCoS</li><li>DLP</li><li>OLED</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microdisplays market comprises several key technologies: </p><p>1. **LCD (Liquid Crystal Display)** utilizes liquid crystals to modulate light, offering good color accuracy and brightness for smaller screens.</p><p> </p><p>2. **LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon)** integrates liquid crystals on a silicon backing, providing high resolution and excellent image quality, but is often more expensive. </p><p>3. **DLP (Digital Light Processing)** uses micro-mirrors for image projection, known for reliability and compactness in projectors.</p><p>4. **OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode)** features self-emissive pixels, delivering vibrant colors and deep blacks, ideal for high-end displays.</p><p>5. **Other technologies** may include technologies like MicroLED or reflective displays, expanding application possibilities across various industries.</p></p>
