<p><p>The AR headset market is generally categorized into two types: integrated and split. Integrated AR headsets combine all components, including processor and display, into a single device worn on the head, offering portability and ease of use. In contrast, split AR headsets separate components, with a lightweight headset connected to an external processing unit, which can enhance performance and battery life. Both types serve different needs, from casual users seeking convenience to professionals needing robust functionality.</p></p>
