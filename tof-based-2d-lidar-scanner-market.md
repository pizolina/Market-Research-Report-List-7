<p><p>The TOF-based 2D Lidar scanner market is divided into two main types: Single-line Mode and Multi-line Mode. Single-line Mode scanners use a single laser beam to capture a single horizontal plane, making them suitable for simpler applications with lower resolution requirements. In contrast, Multi-line Mode scanners employ multiple laser beams to capture multiple horizontal planes simultaneously, offering higher resolution and broader coverage. This makes them ideal for complex environments, allowing for detailed mapping and improved obstacle detection.</p></p>
