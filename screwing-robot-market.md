<p><ul><li>Air Suction Type</li><li>Air Blow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screwing robot market comprises two primary types: air suction and air blow. Air suction type robots use negative pressure to grip and manipulate screws, ensuring a secure hold during assembly processes. In contrast, air blow type robots utilize compressed air to propel screws into designated positions, facilitating rapid insertion. Both technologies enhance efficiency and precision in screw fastening applications across various industries, including manufacturing and assembly, ultimately improving productivity and reducing labor costs.</p></p>
