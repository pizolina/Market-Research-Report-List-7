<p><p>The dyeing auxiliaries market comprises various agents essential for optimizing the dyeing process. Dispersing agents enhance the distribution of dye particles in solution, preventing aggregation. Sequestering agents bind metal ions, preventing unwanted reactions. Scouring agents remove impurities from fabrics, ensuring uniform dye uptake. Wetting agents improve fabric wetting, promoting even dye penetration. Other agents include pH regulators and foaming agents, contributing to overall process efficiency. Each type plays a vital role in achieving high-quality dye results in textiles.</p></p>
