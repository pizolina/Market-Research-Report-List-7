<p><p>The vehicle safety device market is categorized into active and passive safety systems. Active safety systems focus on preventing accidents through technology such as anti-lock braking systems, electronic stability control, and lane departure warnings, enhancing vehicle control and awareness. In contrast, passive safety systems are designed to protect occupants after an accident occurs, featuring airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones that absorb impact energy. Together, these systems work to enhance overall vehicle safety and minimize injuries during collisions.</p></p>
