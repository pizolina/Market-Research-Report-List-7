<p><strong>The Dental Zirconia Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Zirconia Disc</li><li>Zirconia Block</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental zirconia material market primarily includes two types: zirconia discs and zirconia blocks. Zirconia discs are flat, circular pieces used in CAD/CAM systems for creating dental restorations, offering precise fit and strength. Zirconia blocks, on the other hand, are thicker and often used for crafting complex dental structures like crowns, bridges, and frameworks. Both forms are favored for their durability, biocompatibility, and aesthetic appeal, making them essential in modern dental procedures.</p></p>
