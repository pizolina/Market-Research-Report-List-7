<p><p>The heat treatment equipment market comprises various types, primarily focusing on atmosphere and vacuum furnaces. Atmosphere furnaces use controlled environments to prevent oxidation and contamination during heat treatment processes, making them suitable for materials like steel and other alloys. Vacuum furnaces, on the other hand, operate in a vacuum to eliminate atmospheric gases, allowing for high-purity treatments at elevated temperatures. Both types are essential in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for enhancing material properties and performance.</p></p>
