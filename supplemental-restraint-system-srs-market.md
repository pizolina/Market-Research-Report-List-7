<p><p>The Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) market encompasses various safety features designed to enhance occupant protection in vehicles. Key types include driver and passenger front airbags, which deploy in frontal collisions; front seat-mounted airbags, positioned within the seat for side-impact protection; curtain side-impact airbags, which extend from the roof to shield occupants during side collisions; and pretensioners on seatbelts, which tighten the belt upon impact to reduce occupant movement. Together, these components significantly improve safety performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>
