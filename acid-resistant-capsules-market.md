<p><p>The acid-resistant capsules market primarily features two types: Hypromellose (HPMC) and Hypromellose Phthalate (HPMCP). HPMC capsules are made from hydroxypropyl methylcellulose, offering a vegetarian alternative with good moisture resistance and stability. HPMCP, on the other hand, is a derivative designed to withstand acidic environments, protecting sensitive ingredients from degradation in the stomach. Both types cater to the increasing demand for high-quality pharmaceutical and nutraceutical products, providing effective delivery systems for active compounds that require targeted release.</p></p>
