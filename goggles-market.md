<p><strong>The Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption-type</li><li>Reflection-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Goggles can be categorized into absorption-type and reflection-type markets based on their optical properties. Absorption-type goggles use materials that absorb specific wavelengths of light, effectively reducing glare and protecting the eyes from harmful rays. They are commonly used in swimming and skiing. In contrast, reflection-type goggles rely on reflective coatings to bounce light away, enhancing visibility and reducing eye strain in bright conditions. Both types cater to different activities, maximizing comfort and performance in their respective environments.</p></p>
