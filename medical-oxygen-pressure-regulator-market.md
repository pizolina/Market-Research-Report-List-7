<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Two-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical oxygen pressure regulator market is primarily divided into single-stage and two-stage types. Single-stage regulators adjust oxygen pressure in one step, providing a direct and quick supply, often suitable for portable applications. In contrast, two-stage regulators reduce the pressure in two stages, offering more precise control and stability, making them ideal for stationary setups where consistent flow is crucial. Both types ensure safe and efficient oxygen delivery for medical purposes, catering to varying healthcare needs.</p></p>
