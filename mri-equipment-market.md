<p><strong>The MRI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting Type MRI Equipment</li><li>Permanent-magnet Type MRI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI equipment market features two primary types: superconducting type and permanent-magnet type MRI systems. Superconducting MRI machines utilize superconducting magnets cooled to cryogenic temperatures, offering high-resolution imaging and stronger magnetic fields, making them ideal for detailed diagnostics. In contrast, permanent-magnet MRI equipment relies on permanent magnets, providing lower costs, reduced operational complexity, and portability. These systems are typically used in outpatient settings and regions with limited access to advanced technology, catering to diverse clinical needs and budgets.</p></p>
