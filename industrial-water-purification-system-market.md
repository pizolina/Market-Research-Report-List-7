<p><p>The Industrial Water Purification System market comprises three main types: ion exchange, filtration, and distillation. Ion exchange utilizes resin to remove impurities by exchanging harmful ions with benign ones, effectively softening water. Filtration employs various media, like sand or activated carbon, to physically separate contaminants from water. Distillation involves boiling water to create vapor, which is then condensed to remove dissolved solids and impurities. Each method serves distinct industrial needs for maintaining water quality in processes and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
