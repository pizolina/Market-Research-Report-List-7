<p><ul><li>Crank Servo Presses</li><li>Screw Servo Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Servo presses are categorized into two main types: crank servo presses and screw servo presses. Crank servo presses utilize a rotating crank system to convert rotary motion into linear motion, offering precise control over speed and force during operations. Conversely, screw servo presses employ a screw mechanism, providing high rigidity and accuracy, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types are favored for their efficiency and ability to enhance productivity in manufacturing processes, especially in the automotive and metalworking industries.</p></p>
