<p><p>The dolomite mining market is segmented into three main types: Calcium Dolomite, Magnesia Dolomite, and Others. Calcium Dolomite is primarily used in construction, agriculture, and as a flux in iron and steel production due to its calcium carbonate content. Magnesia Dolomite, rich in magnesium, is essential in the production of refractory materials and for environmental applications. The "Others" category includes various specialized dolomite products utilized in chemical manufacturing, glass production, and as filler materials in different industries.</p></p>
