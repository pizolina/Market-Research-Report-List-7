<p><p>The forage harvester market consists of two primary types: pull-type forage harvesters and self-propelled forage harvesters. Pull-type forage harvesters are towed by tractors, making them versatile and cost-effective for smaller operations. In contrast, self-propelled forage harvesters are independent machines designed for efficiency and speed, ideal for large-scale farming. They offer enhanced maneuverability and productivity, featuring advanced technology for optimal performance. Both types play crucial roles in harvesting forage for livestock feed, impacting overall agricultural productivity.</p></p>
