<p><p>The rigid contact lenses market includes two primary types: Rigid Gas Permeable (RGP) lenses and Ortho-K lenses. RGP lenses are durable, oxygen-permeable lenses that provide excellent vision correction and are customizable for various refractive errors. Ortho-K lenses, on the other hand, are specially designed to be worn overnight, gently reshaping the cornea to improve vision during the day without the need for glasses or contact lenses. Both types cater to specific vision needs and preferences, contributing to their growing market popularity.</p></p>
