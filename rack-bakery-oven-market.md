<p><p>The rack bakery oven market can be categorized into electric and steam-based types. In contrast, steam-based ovens incorporate steam as a key component, promoting moisture retention in baked goods and enhancing texture. Both types cater to different baking requirements, with electric ovens often preferred for versatility and steam ovens favored for their ability to produce artisan-style products. Each type serves distinct operational needs in commercial bakeries.</p></p>
