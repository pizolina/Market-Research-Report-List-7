<p><strong>The Gauge Pressure Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Line Gauge Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Remote-Sealed Gauge Pressure Transmitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gauge pressure transmitters market comprises primarily two types: In-Line Gauge Pressure Transmitters and Remote-Sealed Gauge Pressure Transmitters. In-Line transmitters are directly connected to the process system, measuring pressure where fluids flow, ensuring real-time monitoring. Remote-Sealed transmitters, on the other hand, feature a diaphragm that separates the measuring element from the process medium, allowing for accurate measurement in challenging conditions, such as corrosive or high-temperature environments. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, enhancing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
