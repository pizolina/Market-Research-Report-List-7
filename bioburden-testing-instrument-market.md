<p><p>The bioburden testing instrument market includes methods such as the membrane-filtration method and the plate count method. The membrane-filtration method involves filtering a sample through a membrane to capture microorganisms, allowing for quantitative analysis. In contrast, the plate count method involves spreading a diluted sample on agar plates to cultivate and count colony-forming units. Both methods are essential in pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and food industries for ensuring product safety and compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
