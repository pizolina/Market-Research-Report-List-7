<p><p>The UD glass fiber prepreg market consists of two main types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset prepregs involve resins that harden permanently when cured, offering excellent mechanical properties and thermal stability. These are typically used in applications requiring high strength and rigidity. In contrast, thermoplastic prepregs utilize resins that can be reshaped and recycled upon heating, providing flexibility and ease of processing. Both types cater to various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and construction, emphasizing performance and sustainability.</p></p>
