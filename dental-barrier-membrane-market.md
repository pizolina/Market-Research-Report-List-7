<p><strong>The Dental Barrier Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resorbable Dental Barrier Membrane</li><li>Non-Resorbable Dental Barrier Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental barrier membrane market is primarily divided into two types: resorbable and non-resorbable membranes. Resorbable dental barrier membranes are designed to be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for a second surgical procedure for removal. They are often made from materials like collagen or PLA. Non-resorbable dental barrier membranes, on the other hand, remain in place indefinitely, providing long-term support for tissue regeneration. These are typically made from synthetic materials such as ePTFE, offering durability for complex dental procedures.</p></p>
