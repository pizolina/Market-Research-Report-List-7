<p><p>The metrology probe market encompasses various types of precision measuring devices, notably machine tool probes and coordinate measuring machine (CMM) probes. Machine tool probes are integrated into CNC machines to facilitate real-time measurements during machining processes, ensuring accuracy and reducing setup times. CMM probes, on the other hand, are used within coordinate measuring machines for assessing the physical geometrical characteristics of an object, offering high precision and repeatability in quality control and inspection applications. Both are essential for enhancing manufacturing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
