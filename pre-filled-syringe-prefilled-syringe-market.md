<p><p>The pre-filled syringe (PFS) market is categorized into two primary types: glass and plastic pre-filled syringes. Glass PFS are known for their excellent barrier properties, chemical resistance, and compatibility with a wide range of medications, making them ideal for sensitive biologics. Conversely, plastic PFS are lightweight, shatter-resistant, and often more cost-effective, catering to a growing demand for convenience and ease of use in self-administration. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing medication delivery and patient adherence.</p></p>
