<p><p>The photoelectrochemical cell market encompasses various types, primarily including dye-sensitized photovoltaic cells and photoelectrolytic cells. Dye-sensitized photovoltaic cells harness light to generate electricity through a photosensitive dye, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility. Photoelectrolytic cells, on the other hand, utilize solar energy to drive chemical reactions, such as water splitting for hydrogen production. Other emerging technologies in this market include tandem cells and perovskite-based systems, all contributing to advancements in renewable energy and sustainable practices.</p></p>
