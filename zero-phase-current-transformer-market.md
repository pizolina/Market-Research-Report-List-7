<p><ul><li>Split Type</li><li>Through Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zero Phase Current Transformer (ZPCT) market can be categorized into two main types: Split Type and Through Type. Split Type ZPCTs feature a design that allows easy installation around existing conductors, making them suitable for retrofitting in various applications. In contrast, Through Type ZPCTs integrate the conductor within the transformer, providing a more compact solution typically used in new installations. Both types effectively measure zero-sequence currents, enhancing protection and monitoring in electrical systems.</p></p>
