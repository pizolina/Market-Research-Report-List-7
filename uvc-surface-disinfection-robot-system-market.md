<p><p>The UVC Surface Disinfection Robot System market is categorized into two main types: UVC LED type and UVC mercury type. UVC LED robots utilize light-emitting diodes, offering energy efficiency, longer lifespans, and rapid response times while emitting UV-C light to disinfect surfaces. In contrast, UVC mercury robots rely on mercury vapor lamps, producing higher intensity UV-C but with limitations in energy consumption and maintenance challenges. Both types aim to improve hygiene in various environments, including healthcare and public spaces.</p></p>
