<p><p>The fiberglass rebar market is segmented into three primary types: GFRP, BFRP, and CFRP rebar. GFRP (Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer) rebar is widely used for its corrosion resistance and lightweight properties, making it suitable for various construction applications. BFRP (Bamboo Fiber Reinforced Polymer) rebar combines bamboo fibers with polymers, enhancing sustainability and reducing environmental impact. CFRP (Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer) rebar offers superior strength and low weight, often utilized in specialized engineering projects due to its high performance and durability.</p></p>
